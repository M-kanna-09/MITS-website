About the Programme

The MBA in Marketing at MITS College equips students with comprehensive knowledge and skills in market research, consumer behavior, digital marketing, brand management, advertising, sales, and strategic marketing management. This program is tailored for aspiring marketing professionals who aim to excel in the competitive global business environment. Students are trained in the latest marketing tools and techniques, analytics-driven decision making, social media marketing, and international marketing trends. Graduates of this program can pursue careers in areas such as brand management, digital marketing, sales management, market research analysis, advertising, and public relations.
