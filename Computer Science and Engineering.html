About the Programme

The Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at MITS Madanapalle is a comprehensive four-year undergraduate program that equips students with the essential knowledge and skills to excel in the dynamic field of computing and information technology. The program focuses on providing in-depth understanding of computer systems, software development, artificial intelligence, data analytics, cybersecurity, cloud computing, and emerging technologies.

Students gain practical experience through hands-on laboratory sessions, real-time projects, coding competitions, hackathons, internships, and industry collaborations. The CSE curriculum is continuously updated to align with industry trends, offering specializations and electives in areas like Machine Learning, Internet of Things (IoT), Blockchain, and Full Stack Development.

The core subjects include Data Structures, Algorithms, Operating Systems, Database Management Systems, Computer Networks, Web Technologies, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Cloud Computing.

The program’s objective is to produce highly skilled computer science professionals who are capable of designing innovative solutions and contributing to the fast-evolving global IT industry.
