About the Programme

The Master of Technology (M.Tech) in Power Systems at MITS Madanapalle is a two-year postgraduate program that provides advanced knowledge and practical expertise in the generation, transmission, distribution, and utilization of electrical power. The program emphasizes the planning, operation, control, and protection of modern power systems, with a focus on sustainable and smart energy solutions.

The curriculum covers core topics such as Power System Analysis, Power System Protection, Power System Operation and Control, High Voltage Engineering, Smart Grids, Renewable Energy Integration, and Energy Management Systems. The program also trains students in the use of modern power system simulation tools like MATLAB, ETAP, and PSCAD for system design and analysis.

Through industry-oriented projects, internships, laboratory work, and research activities, students develop advanced problem-solving abilities and are equipped to address the challenges of modern power networks, including grid stability, energy efficiency, and integration of renewable energy sources.

Graduates of this program can pursue careers in power generation companies, transmission and distribution utilities, renewable energy sectors, smart grid solutions, energy consulting firms, and academic research.
