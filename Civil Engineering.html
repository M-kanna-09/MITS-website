About the Programme

The Bachelor of Technology in Civil Engineering at MITS Madanapalle is a four-year undergraduate program that focuses on planning, designing, constructing, and maintaining infrastructure projects. Civil Engineering is one of the oldest and most essential engineering disciplines that directly impacts the quality of life by building safe and sustainable structures.

The curriculum covers key areas such as Structural Engineering, Transportation Engineering, Environmental Engineering, Geotechnical Engineering, Water Resources Engineering, and Construction Management. Students also gain hands-on experience through extensive fieldwork, laboratory experiments, industrial visits, and real-time projects.

The program equips students to address modern infrastructure challenges and prepares them for careers in construction companies, public works departments, environmental agencies, and research organizations. The course also emphasizes the use of modern design software like AutoCAD, STAAD Pro, and Primavera for structural and project management applications.

Graduates of this program are well-prepared to pursue higher education or professional certifications and contribute effectively to sustainable infrastructure development worldwide.
