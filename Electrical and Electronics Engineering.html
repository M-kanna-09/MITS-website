About the Programme

The Bachelor of Technology in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) at MITS Madanapalle is a four-year undergraduate program that provides a strong foundation in electrical systems, electronic devices, power generation, transmission, and advanced control systems. The program emphasizes both theoretical principles and hands-on practical training to prepare students for the modern energy and automation sectors.

The EEE curriculum integrates topics such as Electrical Machines, Power Systems, Power Electronics, Control Systems, Renewable Energy Technologies, Embedded Systems, and Electric Drives. Students gain expertise in designing, analyzing, and maintaining electrical and electronic systems, along with exposure to industrial applications and smart grid technologies.

Through laboratory sessions, industrial visits, workshops, internships, and real-time projects, students develop skills to meet the challenges of evolving power and automation industries.

Graduates from this program can pursue careers in power plants, manufacturing industries, renewable energy sectors, electrical equipment design, automation, and research organizations. The program also lays the groundwork for higher studies and professional certifications in electrical engineering.
