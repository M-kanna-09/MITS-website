Welcome to Madanapalle Institute of Technology Science

In recent years, Madanapalle has evolved into a centre of excellence in many ways. Yet, its most prominent identity remains that of a premium educational hub, attracting countless aspirants seeking a successful career. The town is home to a diverse range of academic and professional institutes managed by esteemed educational trusts and organizations that have established themselves under prestigious banners. Among these, Madanapalle Institute of Technology & Science (MITS) stands tall as a beacon of quality education. Established in the year 1998, MITS is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Anantapur (JNTUA) and is recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. Since its inception, MITS has been committed to providing students with a rich and conducive learning environment, enabling them to acquire the essential knowledge and skills required for success in both their professional and personal lives.
