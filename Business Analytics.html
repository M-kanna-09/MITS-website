About the Programme

The MBA in Business Analytics at MITS College is designed to develop analytical and data-driven decision-making skills among future business leaders. This program blends management expertise with modern analytics tools, enabling students to translate complex data into strategic business insights. The curriculum covers areas such as data visualization, predictive analytics, business intelligence, machine learning applications, marketing analytics, financial analytics, and operations analytics. Students learn to leverage analytics tools like Python, R, Tableau, Power BI, and SQL for informed decision-making in various business functions. Graduates from this program can build rewarding careers in fields like data analysis, business consulting, marketing analytics, financial forecasting, and supply chain optimization.
